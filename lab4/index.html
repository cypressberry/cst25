Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

The challenge for this assignment was to create pseudocode for and everyday task and a simple computer game.

Problems

For this assignment, I encountered issues relating to the use of the "pre" tag in HTML in order to format code on the page. In order for the code to be properly rendered, I needed to break tab conventions in the HTML file which my HTML prettier does not like. The problem is that indenting my code block properly results in those exact indentations being translated into the final website which is not ideal seeing as our site does not use the entire width of the page for content to begin with in order to better display our image backgrounds.

Making my Favorite Sandwich at the Dining Hall.

1. Get a plate
	- Obtain a plate from a stack located in the dining hall 
2. Assemble the ingredients on the sandwich in order 
	- Collect 2 slices of buttermilk bread from the bread drawers 
	- Place slices on the plate 
	- Spread mayonnaise and thousand islands dressing on the bread slices 
	- Gather enough onion slices to cover the surface of the bread 
	- Collect 3 tomato slices and cover the onion slices with them 
	- Gather 3 “rolls” of the sliced corned beef and spread evenly on top of the tomato slices 
	- Obtaining a single large piece of lettuce and put it on top of the tomato slices 
	- Finish by laying the second slice of bread on top of the tomato slices dressing side down. 
3. Cook the sandwich for 5 minutes in the panini press 
	- Open the press if it is not already. 
	- Transport the sandwich inside the mouth of the panini press 
	- Pull the lever to close the press 
	- Wait 5 minutes 
	- Push the lever to open the press 
	- Remove the sandwich 
4. Place the sandwich on the plate
